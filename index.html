Local Area Network (LAN) Solutions

Businesses with an internal network of computers, printers, CCTV and telephony devices usually require local area network (LAN) technology. For this purpose, Ethernet cables are generally used.

The composition of Ethernet cables has changed over the years as technology progresses; today's cables are capable of transferring higher volumes of data compared to older cables. With higher internet speeds and increased demands in terms of communication and data transfer, high performance Ethernet cables are essential.

About Us

Northern Territory Communications Pty Ltd (NTC) started business in October 2002 with owners Craig Grose and Kevin MacDonald, who previously formed the management team of International Communications & Electronics NT Pty Ltd (ICE NT) for 6 years. With 35+ years of combined experience, NTC is a TE Connectivity 20-year Endorsed Installer, and Molex & Clipsal lifetime endorsed installer.

We work in secure environments with clients including the Australian Defence Force, Australian Customs Service, NT Police, and Darwin International Airport. In 2008, we joined the Defence Industry Security Program (DISP).

Since 2013, NTC has been a contractor for NBN (MDU projects) and serves as the Darwin regional service agents for AAPT.
